Concept

The concept refers to toxic masculinity and the different experiences and associations that people have and share with the term, the soundtracks run in the background and viewers are invited to take a closer look at the flowers, which are to be explored in an abstract and altered form. With the aim of turning clichés and stereotypes on their head and assigning flowers to the opposite sex.
